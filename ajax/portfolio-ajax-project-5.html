Audiophile Project

I developed Audiophile, a high-end e-commerce site for audio equipment enthusiasts. The website features a sleek, user-friendly interface, showcasing a curated selection of premium headphones, speakers, and earphones. Each product page includes detailed descriptions, high-quality images, and user reviews.
